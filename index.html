About Razorback Storm Chasing

We don't just chase storms‚Äîwe live for them. Every rumble of thunder, every rotation in the clouds, every bolt of lightning tells a story, and we're there to capture it.

Founded by a team of meteorologists, photographers, and adrenaline junkies, Razorback Storm Chasing has spent over a decade pursuing nature's most powerful phenomena across Tornado Alley and beyond. From the Texas Panhandle to the Kansas plains, we've witnessed hundreds of tornadoes, documented countless supercells, and experienced the raw, untamed beauty of severe weather firsthand.

But we're more than just thrill-seekers. We're educators, storytellers, and weather enthusiasts committed to sharing the science and spectacle of storms with the world. Our footage has been featured in major documentaries, news outlets, and educational programs. We provide real-time ground truth data to the National Weather Service, helping meteorologists issue more accurate warnings that save lives.

Whether you're a weather enthusiast looking to join your first chase, a media outlet seeking premium storm footage, or a school wanting to bring severe weather education to your students, we bring professionalism, safety, and passion to everything we do.

Our Mission: To document the power and beauty of severe weather while promoting safety, education, and respect for nature's most incredible displays.
